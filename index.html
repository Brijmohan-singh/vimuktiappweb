Data Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Data

Users of the Vimukti Sanstha Android application can delete their account and associated data by following the steps below:

1

Open the Vimukti Sanstha mobile application

Launch the app on your Android device.

2

Log in to your account

Use your registered email ID and password to log in.

3

Go to the Profile page

After logging in, navigate to the Profile section.

4

Tap on the My Profile button

Access your profile details and settings.

5

Scroll to the bottom of the page

Find the account management options at the bottom.

6

Click on the Delete Account button

This will initiate the account deletion process.

7

Confirm the deletion when prompted

A confirmation dialog will appear to verify your decision.

Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following information will be removed from our systems:

  • User account information - Profile details, contact information, login credentials
  • Attendance records - All your attendance history
  • Location data linked to attendance
Data Retention
Important: Some data may be retained for legal or administrative purposes for up to 90 days after deletion.

After this retention period, all remaining data will be permanently purged from our systems.

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any issues during the deletion process or have questions about your data, please contact our app administrator.

Note: This process is only applicable to users of the Vimukti Sanstha Android application.
